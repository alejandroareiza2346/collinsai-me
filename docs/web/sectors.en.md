# Target Sectors — CollinsAI

We focus on SMBs with manual operations that require operational scaling:

- Healthcare (clinics, practices): appointment management, reminders, payment reconciliation.
- Real Estate: lead handling, visit scheduling, buyer follow-up.
- Ecommerce & Retail: order processing, shipping notifications, inventory reconciliation.
- Legal & Accounting: document tracking, deadline reminders, client management.
- Professional Services (consultants, freelancers): invoicing, proposal follow-up, lightweight CRM.

For each sector we define: pain points, minimum viable automation flow, operational KPIs and standard deliverables.
