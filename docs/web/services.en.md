# Services — CollinsAI (Phase 1: Agency)

## Core Services

### Operational Process Automation

- Process mapping, scripts and n8n flows to remove manual work.
- Examples: payment reconciliation, invoice generation, inventory sync.

### Automated CRM and Sales Follow-up

- Pipeline automation triggered from forms, WhatsApp and email.
- Automated contact tasks, reminders and lead scoring.

### Conversational AI for WhatsApp

- Bots for pre-qualification, FAQ handling, human escalation and reminders.
- Secure integration with CRM and audit logs.

### Operational Dashboards & Reporting

- Connectors to PostgreSQL and Redis; panels with KPIs, alerts and scheduled reports.

### API Integrations & Connectors

- Secure connectors to ERPs, payment gateways and external providers.

### AI Agents for Operational Tasks

- Agents that execute actions (e.g., process orders, send notifications).

### Consulting & Security

- Process audits, data governance and infrastructure hardening (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options).

## Delivery Model

- Operational diagnostic → Automation MVP (2–6 weeks) → Iterate and monitor.
- Project-based delivery with measurable milestones and impact reports (time saved, leads recovered).
