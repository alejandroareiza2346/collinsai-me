## What CollinsAI is

CollinsAI is a company focused on intelligent automation, artificial intelligence integration, and operational optimization for small and medium-sized businesses in Colombia and Latin America.

The company aims to help traditional businesses modernize their operations through process automation, intelligent systems, and AI tools applied to real problems.

## Main objective

To become the leading Automation Intelligence company for SMBs in Colombia and Latin America.
